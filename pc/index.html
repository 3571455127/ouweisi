About Us

Established in 2015, Jiangmen Meifang Biotechnology co., LTD is located in the natio al high-tech development zone of Jiangmen City. Continuing the path of aromatherapy and health preservation, and committed to promoting the purpose of plant pharmacology and aromatherapy washing and nursing. We are a modern enterprise integrating science, industry and trade. Meifang company adopts the international standard GMP dust-free workshop, professional ERP management mode. And the production system is divided into four workshops: emulsification room, soap making room, filling room and packaging room, covering an area of 8000 square meters.
